About Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahabad

The Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahabad (IIITA) is a premier institute of excellence in Information Technology and allied areas. Established in 1999, IIITA offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines.

Located in Allahabad, Uttar Pradesh, India, IIITA is known for its world-class faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and innovative research facilities. The institute is committed to nurturing talent, fostering creativity, and promoting excellence in the field of Information Technology.
